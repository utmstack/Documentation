## Installing Cisco ASA

### 1. Configure your device to send logs to a UTMStack agent on ports:
        * 1470 TCP
        * 514 UDP

### 2. Enable log collector.


> **Note:** To enable the log collector where you have the UTMStack agent installed, follow the instructions below based on your operating system and preferred protocol.
